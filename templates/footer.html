About the Developer

OneDrop was created by Hazrat Ummar Shaikh, an independent Android developer from West Bengal, India. Driven by a passion for real-world impact, Hazrat designed OneDrop to be a simple, transparent platform connecting blood donors and recipients. His commitment to user privacy and a minimalist approach ensures a focused and authentic experience. Learn more at hazratdev.top
